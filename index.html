MY EXPERIENCE AT JOBSTEMS

I worked closely with Development and designing teams, right from the requirement gathering stages, to designing of logo, to the testing phase of each website page. During this time, I was responsible to do paper prototyping and laying down wireframes using microsoft visio.

I was constantly interacting with senior designers to get valuable suggestions and responded them back to development team iteratively until we got a perfect design as an implementable web page.

To help development understand our design concepts, we also developed basic HTML, CSS pages and gave it to the development team for further addition of functionalities. I was also involved with web page testing which involved testing of browser compatibility of web pages, basically we tested whether the web pages are functioning as intended on different browsers.

We also checked responsiveness of web pages using emulators, and checked if they fit on different screen resolutions.


During this time, I was highly inspired to take up UI designing as my career and hence I decided to pursue Masters in this track to gain more knowledge in this domain.
